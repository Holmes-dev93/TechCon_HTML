History of TechCon

TechCon was founded in 2018 by a small group of visionary developers aiming to bridge the gap between academic research and industry application. What started as a local meetup has evolved into one of the world's most influential technology conferences. Our first event, held in a small auditorium, featured just 12 speakers. Today, we host thousands of attendees and hundreds of sessions across multiple tracks.

Each year, we strive to bring fresh perspectives and disruptive ideas to the forefront of the technology discussion.

Our Mission

The driving principle of TechCon is to **empower innovation and foster inclusion** within the global tech community. We aim to:

  • **Inspire:** Present ground-breaking research and future-forward concepts.
  • **Connect:** Create networking opportunities between emerging talent and established industry leaders.
  • **Educate:** Offer practical workshops and deep-dive sessions on the latest tools and frameworks.
  • **Diversify:** Ensure a platform where voices from all backgrounds and experiences are heard and valued.

Notable Past Speakers

A leading AI Ethicist who presented a keynote on "Decolonizing Data: Building Fairer Machine Learning Models." Her work has fundamentally shifted how major tech companies approach bias in datasets.

Kenji is renowned for his contributions to quantum-resistant encryption. He shared practical insights on securing legacy systems against future cryptographic threats, emphasizing proactive security measures.
